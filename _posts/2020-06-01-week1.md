My first week was focused on establishing the scope of the project as well as familiarizing myself with literature on hearing loss, hearing aids, and hearing aid apps.The most important thing learned from these articles thus far is that hearing aids don't fully restore hearing due. The goals our team has established is to first research and test all the current tools out there; we want to understand the space we're working in.  We then want to conduct a qualitative study to see what hearing aid users like about the functionality of their hearing aid apps and what they feel is lacking and finally try and develop the beginnings of a mock application to address the needs we find. Some challenges this week have been narrowing down the methods of the study and what sort of questions we would like to ask and how we want to ask them. I am beginning to find literature on the topic of qualitative studies and creating.
