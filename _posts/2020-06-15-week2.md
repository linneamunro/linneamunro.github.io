We have now decided on collecting the experiences users are having with hearing aid apps with interviews. We are focusing the UI and UX designs of apps and seeing if there is any parts of the design that can be improved. After our first round of interviews we will be taking this feedback and creating a mock app interface that addressing the needs we discover. We will then do a second round of interviews to see if we made any progress in removing some barriers. Some foreseeable problems seem to be the transition for older adults to integrate the app into their experience, apps crashing and disconnecting, as well as the lack of tools useful enough to justify accepting these inconveniences. Some challenges this week has been building a schedule around IRB application and approval and fitting the whole project in this small time frame. Otherwise we seem to be on track to discover some really interesting things.
