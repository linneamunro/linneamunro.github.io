## About My Mentor

Raja Kushalnagar is the Director of the Information Technology program in the Department of Science, Technology and Mathematics at Gallaudet University in Washington, DC. He has done over 10 years of accumulated experience working in accessible computing design. His research interests encompass the fields of accessible computing and accessibility/intellectual property law, with the goal of improving information access for deaf and hard of hearing individuals.

## About My Project

My project is to assess the experience hearing aid users have with their hearing aid smartphone applications. Through qualitative methods I will seek out both the benefits and downsides of the UI/UX designs and the tools of the major apps available. Using this info I will then design a basic UI/UX that will hopefully address the needs we discover with the first evaluations. We will then host a second round of interviews and surveys to gain feedback.
